About Me

My name is James, I make cinematic short films of different locations and use my editing skills I have learned over the years to create impactful videos. I have over 5 years of experience editing videos and films. I also enjoy Metalworking and Woodworking alongside my filmmaking. Combining creativity with technical skills allows me to produce unique content that tells compelling visual stories.I am always looking for ways to improve and people to collab with. If you are interested you can find out how to contact me at the bottom of the page.
